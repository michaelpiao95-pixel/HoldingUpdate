## 2. What happened this quarter
MindWalk Holdings (formerly ImmunoPrecise Antibodies) is an AI-driven biotherapeutics / antibody-discovery company. Q3 FY2026 revenue rose 52% YoY to $4.2M — a third straight quarter of year-over-year growth — with US revenue doubling to ~$2.6M and gross margin at 59%. Losses narrowed. Revenue is lumpy for this kind of services/discovery business (Q1 FY2026, ended July 2025, was a record $7.6M, larger than Q3), so trailing-twelve-months and YoY-quarter comparisons matter more than sequential.

The rebrand from ImmunoPrecise to MindWalk signals a strategic repositioning around AI/computational biology in drug discovery — the growth and margin expansion suggest the pivot is resonating commercially.
