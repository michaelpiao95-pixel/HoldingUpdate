# MTY Food Group — Q1 Fiscal 2026 Earnings (extract)

## Income statement (Q1 FY2026 vs Q1 FY2025)
- Revenue: $267.8M vs $284.8M (-6.0% YoY)
- System sales: ~$1.3B
- Segment profit / Adjusted EBITDA: $59.8M (normalized $60.1M), ~22% of revenue (+2% YoY)
- Net income attributable to owners: $36.9M vs $1.7M
- Diluted EPS: $1.62 vs $0.07
  NOTE: the huge YoY EPS jump is mainly because prior-year Q1 2025 was depressed by
  impairment/non-cash charges — this is a base-effect, not 20x operational growth.

## Operating metrics
- Same-store sales: -2.5% overall; Canada -0.8%; US -3.6%; International -1.3%
- Locations: 52 openings, 90 closings → net -38 (plus an 8-store TCBY reduction);
  total 7,034 locations (57% US / 35% Canada / 8% International)

## Cash flow & balance sheet
- Operating cash flow: $40.9M (vs $64.6M PY)
- Free cash flow net of lease payments: $29.0M
- Long-term debt: $605.1M; cash $56.0M
- Revolver drawn: C$250.0M + US$262.0M

## Capital return
- Quarterly dividend: $0.37/share (payable 2026-05-15)

## Management quotes (CEO Eric Lefebvre)
1. "Our asset-light, well diversified model continues to demonstrate its strong cash flow
   profile despite persistent macro economic headwinds."
2. "We believe store locations will be a bright spot for 2026, continuing the trend from the
   second half of 2025."
