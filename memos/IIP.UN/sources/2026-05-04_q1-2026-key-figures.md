# InterRent REIT — Q1 2026 (key figures) + PRIVATIZATION

## Q1 2026 operating results
- Occupancy: 96.3% (March; total & same-property), −50 bps YoY
- Average monthly rent: $1,767 (+2.6% total; +1.5% same-property)
- Same-property proportionate NOI: $38.9M (+1.0% YoY); NOI margin 64.1% (stable)
- FFO/unit: $0.05 (down from $0.15 in Q1 2025); total FFO $6.98M (−68% YoY)
  → FFO/AFFO hit by $13.3M one-time transaction costs related to the Arrangement Agreement (privatization)
- Gain-on-lease: 3.1% on 414 new leases

## ⚠ CORPORATE — PRIVATIZATION COMPLETED
- InterRent agreed to be acquired by Carriage Hill Properties Acquisition Corp. (CLV Group + GIC) for ~$4B all-cash, $13.55/unit (announced ~2025-05; ~35% premium to prior close).
- Statutory plan of arrangement (Ontario BCA); required 2/3 unitholder approval; Investment Canada Act approval obtained.
- COMPLETED 2026-07-09 — InterRent delisted from TSX, ceasing to be a reporting issuer.
- Implication for portfolio: this position has effectively been cashed out at $13.55/unit; no longer a live public holding.
