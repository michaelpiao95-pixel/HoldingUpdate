# Alibaba — March Quarter FY2026 (key figures)
Report date: 2026-05-12 (US)/2026-05-13. Period: quarter ended 2026-03-31 (Alibaba's fiscal Q4 FY2026; FY ends Mar 31). Source: "Alibaba Group Announces March Quarter 2026 and Fiscal Year 2026 Results" (saved: 2026-05-12_marchqtr-fy2026-press-release.pdf), pdftotext. IR: https://www.alibabagroup.com/en-US/ir-financial-reports ; SEC 6-K: https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/0001577552/000119312526274928/d133513dex991.pdf

## Quarter (ended 2026-03-31)
- Revenue: RMB243,380M (US$35,283M), +3% YoY
- Loss from operations: RMB(848)M (US$(123)M) vs income RMB28,465M PY — driven by decrease in adjusted EBITA
- Adjusted EBITA (non-GAAP): −84% YoY (heavy AI/cloud + quick-commerce investment)
- Net income attributable to ordinary shareholders: RMB25,476M (US$3,693M); net income RMB23,502M (+96% YoY, boosted by investment gains)
- Diluted EPS/ADS: RMB10.36 (US$1.50); Non-GAAP diluted EPS/ADS RMB0.62 (US$0.09), −95% YoY
- Operating cash flow: ~RMB9,360M, −66% vs RMB27,520M PY
- Free cash flow: OUTFLOW RMB(17,300)M (US$(2,508)M) — quick commerce, Qwen app user acquisition, cloud capex
- Cloud Intelligence Group: external revenue +40%; AI-related product revenue triple-digit growth (11th straight qtr); AI = 30% of cloud external revenue
- China e-commerce customer management revenue +8% like-for-like

## Full year FY2026
- Revenue: RMB1,023,670M (US$148,401M), +3% YoY
- Income from operations: RMB50,150M (US$7,270M), −64% YoY

Note: near-term margins/EBITA/FCF crushed by deliberate AI+cloud+quick-commerce investment; headline net income up on non-operating investment gains. Read on adjusted EBITA / non-GAAP EPS, not GAAP net income.
