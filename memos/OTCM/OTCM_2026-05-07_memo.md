# OTC Markets Group Inc. (OTCM) — Earnings Memo
Period: Q1 FY2026 | Report Date: ~2026-05-07 [date approximate] | Prepared: 2026-07-13

## 1. Snapshot
- Revenue: $34.8M gross (YoY +14%) — source: OTC Markets Q1 2026 release
- EPS: GAAP diluted $0.59 (+18% YoY) — net income $7.1M (+17%); operating income $8.6M (+19%)
- One-line takeaway: Strong, high-quality quarter — double-digit revenue growth led by OTC Link (+31%), margin expansion, steady dividend and buybacks. A capital-light compounder firing on all cylinders.

## 2. What happened this quarter
OTC Markets operates trading (OTC Link ATS), market-data, and corporate-services businesses — a capital-light, recurring-revenue model. Q1 gross revenue rose 14% to $34.8M with growth across the board: OTC Link revenues +31% (trading/connectivity, likely aided by active markets and subscriber growth), and Corporate Services +19% (issuer subscriptions to OTCQX/OTCQB). That top-line strength dropped through to 19% operating-income growth ($8.6M) and 17% net-income growth ($7.1M), with diluted GAAP EPS up 18% to $0.59 — evidence of operating leverage in the model.

(An "adjusted EPS of $0.93 / 52% beat" figure appears in a secondary summary; the gap versus GAAP $0.59 suggests a one-time item and it isn't confirmed from the primary release — treat cautiously [UNVERIFIED].)
