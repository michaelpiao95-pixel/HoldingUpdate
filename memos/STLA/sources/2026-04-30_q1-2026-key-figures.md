- Net revenues: €38.1B, +6% YoY
- Consolidated shipments: 1,361k units, +12% YoY
- Net profit: €0.4B (€377M) — return to profitability
- Adjusted operating income (AOI): €1.0B; AOI margin 2.5%
- Industrial free cash flow: €(1.9)B (−€1.921B), improved ~37% YoY
- Industrial available liquidity: €44.1B (~28% of trailing-12m revenues)
- Regions: North America sales +6%, share 7.9% (+80 bps); Enlarged Europe sales +5% (+8% incl. Leapmotor), EU30 share 17.5% (+20 bps)
- FY2026 guidance: net revenues mid-single-digit % increase; AOI margin low-single-digit %; industrial FCF improved YoY (positive expected in 2027)
- CEO Antonio Filosa: "The first three months of 2026 reflect early results of our actions to return Stellantis to sustainable, profitable growth."
